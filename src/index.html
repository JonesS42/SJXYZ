About Me

My name is Sabrina and I live in Portland, Oregon. Most recently I've worked as a database admin for a blockchain NFT marketplace and prior to that at an advertising and telecom company. Additionally I have experience in the realm of freelance blockchain data analytics. In parallel I've also worked as a 3D artist and animator for a mobile game studio. Throughout my career I've thrived in the many startup ecosystems I've been a part of and leveraged my understanding of agile development frameworks to deliver impactful results. I've always been captivated by emerging technologies and quite enjoy the way they often require unique solutions for problems that haven't existed before. In my downtime I enjoy exploring the culinary arts, honing my skills in 3D artistry, immersing myself in video games, connecting with nature on hikes, and attending live music events. These hobbies not only provide balance to my life but also fuel my creativity and problem-solving abilities.
