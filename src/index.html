About Me

My name is Sabrina but I also go by Cryptotoad, I'm 30 and living in Portland, Oregon. I've recently worked as a database admin at an advertising and telecommunications company and also as a 3d artist and animator for a mobile game studio. I have an affinity for music, video games, baking, and hiking. I first found out about Bitcoin in 2011 but didn't start trading crypto until 2020 and just recently became captivated by NFTs in the Summer of 2021. I have a lot of passion for cryptocurrency and am excited by all the possible use cases for it and the way those might allow us to decentralize democracy and improve the world.
