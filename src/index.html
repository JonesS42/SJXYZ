About Me

My name is Sabrina, I'm 31 and living in Portland, Oregon. I've recently worked as a database admin for a blockchain NFT marketplace and previously at an advertising and telecom company. I also have experience doing freelance blockchain data analytics. On a different note I've also worked as a 3D artist and animator for a mobile game studio. Much of my employment has been for startups and I have a deep understanding of different agile development frameworks. I've always been captivated by emerging technologies and quite enjoy the way they often require unique solutions for problems that haven't existed before. I have an affinity for music, video games, baking, 3D art, and hiking so when I'm not working you can usually find me doing something related to one of these hobbies.
