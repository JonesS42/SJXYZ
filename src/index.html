About Me

My name is Sabrina but I also go by Cryptotoad, I'm 31 and living in Portland, Oregon. I've recently worked as a database admin for a blockchain NFT marketplace and previously at an advertising and telecom company. I also have experience doing freelance blockchain data analytics. On a different note I've also worked as a 3D artist and animator for a mobile game studio. Much of my employment has been for startups and I have a deep understanding of different agile development frameworks. I first found out about Bitcoin in 2011 but didn't start trading crypto until 2020 and became captivated by NFTs in the Summer of 2021. I have a lot of passion for cryptocurrency and am excited by all the possible use cases for it and the way those might allow us to decentralize democracy and improve the world. I've always been captivated by emerging technologies and quite enjoy the way they often require unique solutions for problems that haven't existed before. I have an affinity for music, video games, baking, 3D art, and hiking so when I'm not working you can usually find me doing something related to one of these.
